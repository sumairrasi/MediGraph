# Clear Clinic  

<img src="assets\images\photo.jpeg" alt="Alt Text" width="600" height="600">

**Clear Clinic** is an AI-powered conversational chatbot designed to identify potential diseases based on symptoms provided by users. It provides comprehensive insights into medical conditions, including causes, diagnosis, treatments, and preventive measures.  

---

## 🚀 Features  
- **Conversational Chatbot**: Interactive and user-friendly AI assistant for symptom-based queries.  
- **Graph RAG Integration**: Combines graph databases with retrieval-augmented generation to ensure accurate and contextually relevant information.  
- **Detailed Medical Insights**: Comprehensive details about diseases, such as causes, symptoms, diagnostic methods, treatment plans, and prevention.    

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used  
- **Conversational AI**: Powered by OpenAI and Agents.  
- **Graph RAG**: For structured and accurate knowledge retrieval.  
  

---

## 📌 Goals  
- Provide accessible, reliable, and personalized healthcare insights.  
- Empower users with early awareness and actionable health information.  
- Bridge the gap between users and preliminary health knowledge.  

---

## 📝 Future Enhancements  
- **Dynamic Medical Updates**: Integration with live databases for real-time updates on diseases and treatments.  
- **Multilingual Support**: Expanding accessibility by supporting multiple languages.
